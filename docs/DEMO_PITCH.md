# Demo Day 1-Minute Pitch (English)

> Target: 0G APAC Hackathon Demo Day, HK Web3 Festival, April 22.
> Audience: judges, 0G core team, VCs, other builders.
> Length: ~60 seconds · ~160 words · medium pace.

---

## Script

**[Look at the stage/camera. Confident, steady pace.]**

Hi, I'm Small. I built **FX Risk Agent** — an AI risk-monitoring agent for cross-border payments where **every decision is a public, verifiable on-chain artifact**.

Today most AI systems are black boxes. When a bank's AI flags a transaction, no one can audit *what the AI actually knew and judged at that moment*. That gap is fatal for regulated finance.

FX Risk Agent closes it. Every time my AI analyzes a currency pair, the full reasoning — model metadata, confidence, recommendation — gets written to **0G Storage**. The alert hits **0G Chain**. The agent itself is tokenized as an **ERC-7857 INFT**. And the inference runs on **0G Compute** with TEE verification.

**All four 0G components. Live on Galileo today.**

The Dashboard fetches decision logs **directly from 0G Storage in the browser** — no backend, no middleman. That's what Verifiable AI actually looks like.

GitHub, live Dashboard, and demo video are all in the HackQuest page. **Thank you.**
